Brief

Nexchannel is an ambitious project to improve the way people enjoy video content online.

Process

We joined Nexchannel early in the process and helped build and form a strong team. We developed the architecture needed to capture and transcode an asset-heavy product. We then provided extensive API development to include file uploads, transcoding, and scheduling. Performance and high speed were critical to this product--which demanded solutions from theoretical computer science to achieve. Extensive use of immutable deployment, continuous delivery, and Amazon Web Services were involved.

Result

Nexchannel is presently in beta testing. We look forward to seeing how the market takes to this product. Our services provide a high performance and high scale basis.
